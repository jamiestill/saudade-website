The boat

SV Saudade is a 44-foot sailing catamaran helmed by her owner, Jamie Still. She operates on the US East Coast, Flordia Gulf Coast, Caribbean, and Bahamas.

Features

  • 3 × cabins, w/ ensuite bathrooms (heads)
  • 2 × 55hp Volvo Penta diesels
  • North Sails Code Zero, Parasailor (spinnaker)
  • 5kW generator
  • 1200ah lithium battery bank and 5000w inverter+charger
  • Air conditioning
  • Dive compressor
  • 2 × the hulls for twice the fun
