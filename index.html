sau·da·de
/sauˈdaːd(ᶾ)i;/ n. Portuguese
  • 1 a longing or desire for a beloved thing
  • 2 name of this sailing catamaran
— see freedom; liberty.

Saudade is a 44-foot (13.4 meter) Fountaine Pajot Helia sailing catamaran. She's plyed the waters of the Eastern Caribbean, the Bahamas, the US East Coast, Florida Keys, and western Florida.

Features

  1. Three cabins, including a master stateroom
  2. Three en suite bathrooms (heads)
  3. Dive compressor
  4. 1200 amp hour Lithium battery bank
  5. 11 foot tender with 20 horsepower engine
  6. Code zero and spinnaker
  7. Solar panels, 900 watts
  8. 5kW Northern Lights generator
  9. Spectra Catalina watermaker
