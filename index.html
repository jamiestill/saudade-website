The Fountaine-Pajot H√©lia

I bought Saudade (then called Liberty Blue) in Saint Martin, West Indies (SXM) in November 2015 and have been sailing her since August 2016. She's my home and gives me the freedom to travel to different parts of the world whilst bringing my home along with me.

She's a Fountaine-Pajot H√©lia, made by the Fountaine-Pajot company in La Rochelle, France. These boats are a favorite among cruisers, charterers, and those who like to sail flat. Several successful YouTube sailing and lifestyle channels feature the H√©lia, and the model is extremely popular in the brokerage market.

The boat is simple to sail, even when short on crew (or one crew), and is a real joy when the wind freshens. The expansive deck space offers many lounging options and additional safety at sea—Saudade comes complete with all the safety features you'd expect of blue water sailboat. And the gracious galley, big beds and heads, and voluminous storage help make my home a home.
