The Boat

SV Saudade is a 44-foot sailing catamaran helmed by her owner, Jamie Still. She operates on the US East Coast, Florida Gulf Coast, Caribbean, and Bahamas.

Features

  • 2 × 55hp Volvo Penta diesels
  • North Sails Code Zero
    Parasailor (spinnaker)
  • 5kW generator
  • 1200ah lithium (LiFePO4) battery bank
    5000w inverter+charger
  • 3 × cabins
    —w/ ensuite bathrooms (heads)
  • Air conditioning and heating
  • 900w of solar panels
  • Multiple paddleboards
  • Dive compressor
  • 20hp powered RIB tender
  • An endless list of things to fix
  • 2 × the hulls for 2 × the fun
